## Page Hierarchy & URLs

### Primary Pages (Level 1)
- `/` - Homepage
- `/services` - Services overview
- `/case-studies` - All case studies
- `/about` - About page
- `/contact` - Contact form
- `/resources` - Resource hub

### Industry Pages (Level 2)
- `/google-ads-for-hvac`
- `/google-ads-for-plumbing`
- `/google-ads-for-electrical`
- `/google-ads-for-roofing`
- `/google-ads-for-landscaping`

- `/resources/[category]/[post-slug]`
- Example: `/resources/hvac/hvac-lead-generation-strategies`

### Utility Pages
- `/privacy-policy`
- `/terms-of-service`

## Call-to-Action (CTA) Strategy

### Primary CTA
**"Get Your Free Audit"** / **"Claim Your Free Audit"**
- Appears on: Every page (header button + in-page CTAs)
- Links to: Contact page
- Color: Blue (primary brand color)

### Secondary CTA
**"View Case Studies"** / **"See Results"**
- Appears on: Homepage, Services page
- Links to: Case studies page
- Color: Gray (secondary)

### Tertiary CTA
**"Schedule Consultation"** / **"Book Your Call"**
- Appears on: About, Contact, Case Studies
- Links to: Contact page or Calendly
- Color: Blue (primary)

## Mobile-First Considerations

### Essential Mobile Features
- Tap-to-call buttons on all pages
- Simplified navigation (hamburger menu)
- Sticky header with CTA button
- Mobile-optimized forms
- Fast loading images
- Readable font sizes (16px minimum)
- Touch-friendly buttons (44px minimum)

### Mobile Page Priorities
1. Homepage (primary entry point)
2. Services page (conversion page)
3. Contact page (lead capture)
4. Industry pages (SEO entry points)
5. Case studies (social proof)

- H1: "Turn Google Ads Into A Predictable Lead Machine"

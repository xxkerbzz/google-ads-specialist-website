## SEO Optimization Strategy

### Pillar Page Optimization
- **Title Tag**: "[Industry] Google Ads | Generate Quality Leads | LeadGen Ads"
- **Meta Description**: "Expert Google Ads management for [industry] businesses. Generate qualified leads with proven strategies. See real results and pricing."
- **H1**: "Google Ads for [Industry]: [Benefit]"
- **H2s**: Challenges, Strategies, Services, Results, Process, FAQ
- **Word Count**: 3,000-3,500 words
- **Schema**: Article, Service, FAQPage

### Supporting Article Optimization
- **Title Tag**: "[Specific Topic] | Google Ads for [Industry]"
- **Meta Description**: 155-160 characters, include primary keyword + benefit
- **H1**: Matches primary keyword intent
- **H2-H6**: Logical hierarchy covering subtopics
- **Word Count**: 1,400-2,200 words
- **Schema**: Article, HowTo (when applicable)

### Image Optimization
- Alt text with industry + topic keywords
- File names: industry-topic-description.jpg
- Compressed for fast loading
- Include infographics, charts, before/after examples

## Conversion Elements

### Every Industry Pillar Includes:
1. **Hero Section**: Industry-specific headline + CTA
2. **Challenge Section**: 4-6 industry-specific pain points
3. **Solution Section**: How we solve these challenges
4. **Service Cards**: Relevant services for industry
5. **Case Study Callout**: Real results from industry
6. **FAQ Section**: 5-8 industry-specific questions
7. **CTA Section**: Free industry-specific audit offer
8. **Related Resources**: Links to supporting content

### Every Supporting Article Includes:
1. **Table of Contents**: For long-form content
2. **Key Takeaways Box**: 3-5 main points (top of article)
3. **In-Content CTAs**: Every 800-1000 words
4. **Related Articles**: 3-4 links to related content
5. **Author Bio**: Brief expert credentials
6. **Comments/Questions Section**: Engagement opportunity
7. **Social Sharing Buttons**: Amplification
8. **Exit-Intent Popup**: Newsletter or free resource

## Internal Linking Strategy

### Hub & Spoke Model
Each main topic (1-8) acts as a hub with spoke pages linking back to it.

**Example:**
- Hub: "Google Ads for HVAC Companies"
- Spokes: HVAC Lead Generation, HVAC Keywords, HVAC Seasonal Tips, etc.
- All spokes link back to hub
- Hub links to all spokes
- Cross-linking between related spokes

### Cross-Silo Linking
Connect related topics across silos:
- Industry pages → Campaign type pages
- Budget pages → Industry pages
- Conversion optimization → Landing pages
- Case studies → Industry & strategy pages

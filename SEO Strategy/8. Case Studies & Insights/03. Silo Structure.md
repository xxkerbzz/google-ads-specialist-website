# Silo Structure: Case Studies & Industry Insights
## Real Results and Industry Knowledge

**Silo Purpose:** Showcase results, provide industry updates, and establish thought leadership.

---


---

## Cross-Silo Linking
- Case studies → Link to relevant industry pages
- Updates → Link to affected campaign types
- Benchmarks → Link to budget planning
- All content → Strong CTAs to services and contact

---

## Content Approach
- **Case Studies**: 800-1,200 words each, detailed results
- **Updates**: 600-1,000 words, timely and actionable
- **Benchmarks**: 1,200-1,800 words, data-rich, updated annually
- **Insights**: 1,000-1,500 words, opinion leadership

---

**Estimated Content:** 25+ articles (growing monthly), 25,000-35,000 total words
**Target Traffic:** 3,000+ visitors/month by month 12
**Purpose:** Social proof, authority building, repeat visitors
